Vintage BBQ Buns Recipe

Description...

These Vintage BBQ Buns are soft, fluffy, and perfect for holding your favorite barbecue fillings. Made from scratch with simple ingredients, they bring a nostalgic touch to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the melted butter and the yeast mixture.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide it into 12 equal pieces. Shape each piece into a bun and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Cover the buns with a damp cloth and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush the tops of the buns with beaten egg for a shiny finish.
  8. Bake the buns for 15-20 minutes or until they are golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack before serving.

Enjoy your homemade Vintage BBQ Buns!
